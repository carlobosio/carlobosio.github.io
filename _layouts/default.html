Carlo Bosio

Hi, I am Carlo! I am a PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Mark W. Mueller within the Berkeley AI Research Lab. My research lies at the intersection of Robotics, Multi-Agent Systems and Machine Learning.

I have had the pleasure of studying in Italy, Switzerland, China and the US. Before coming to Berkeley, I obtained an MS with honors in Robotics from Sant'Anna School of Advanced Studies and the University of Pisa, Italy.

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to have a chat!
